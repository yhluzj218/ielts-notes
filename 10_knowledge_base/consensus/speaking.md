## Consensus Principles

### 1. Natural Communication Over Exam Technique

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Principle | Speak naturally and genuinely; do not perform or demonstrate exam skill |
| Agreement | ✅ Joe + Matt + Simon |
| Joe | Core message from first lesson: "just talk, don't perform" |
| Matt | Confirmed: rehearsed-sounding answers hurt the score |
| Simon | Anti-pattern: optimising language before method; authentic performance is the goal |
| Sprint implication | Do not introduce frameworks that make answers sound scripted |
