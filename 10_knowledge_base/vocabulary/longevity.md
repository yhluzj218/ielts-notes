# Vocabulary — longevity

## Front

The quality of lasting or living for a very long time.

## Back

### Word

**longevity**

### Pronunciation

US /lɑːnˈdʒɛvɪti/, UK /lɒnˈdʒɛvɪti/

### Part of Speech

Noun

### Frequency

Listed on Vocabulary.com's "100 Top SAT Words" list.

### Examples

- **Health:** Regular exercise and a balanced diet are widely believed to improve longevity.
- **Society:** The company's longevity in a competitive market comes from its willingness to keep adapting its products.

### Other Forms

- (root: long)
