## Expression

have a greater awareness of

### Function

Describe an increased understanding or recognition of a subject, issue, or group.

### Usage

Use this expression when comparing how well people understand something now with
their understanding in the past, or when contrasting two groups. It is neutral to
formal and works naturally in both IELTS Writing and Speaking.

### Common Collocations

- have a greater awareness of cultural differences
- have a greater awareness of environmental issues
- have a greater awareness of mental health
- develop a greater awareness of social inequality

### Example

Young people tend to have a greater awareness of mental health than previous
generations.
