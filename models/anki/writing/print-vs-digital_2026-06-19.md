## [Vocab] 單字

**Front:** You want to say that a reader physically marked up their book — underlining, adding margin notes — to help them study or remember content. What verb do you use?
**Back:** annotate (v.) — *She annotated every chapter before the exam.*

---

**Front:** You're describing digital content that a company locks behind a subscription fee, making it inaccessible without payment. What's the single word for this barrier?
**Back:** paywall (n.) — *Much of their online content has been placed behind a paywall.*

---

**Front:** You want to describe organising notes or a textbook using different colours to distinguish categories or importance levels. What verb phrase captures this?
**Back:** colour code (v.) — *Students often colour code their notes to highlight key information.*
