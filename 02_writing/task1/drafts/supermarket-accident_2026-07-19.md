# Writing Draft

日期：2026-07-19
類型：Task 1（GT 投訴信；Joe 7/16 作業）

## 題目

You were hurt in a minor accident inside a supermarket, and you wish to make a complaint. Write a letter to the supermarket manager. In your letter
- say who you are and when the accident happened
- give details about the accident
- and say what you want the manager to do.

## 我的草稿

Dear manager,

I hope this letter finds you well. I live nearby and as a regular customer, i usually come to this shop to buy some food everyday. Unfortunately I just had my first accident in this place yesturday, because of the pizza heating oven.

It happened when I tried to reach the refreshments near the pizza heating oven. The ovan was extreme hot and without any protections. Since it was really close to the freshments, I got burn by it accidently. After I had injured, I asked the crew for the first aid, but I was told they did not have medical service. It turned out I bought some ice cube to ease the pain and left with no further assistence.

I hightlighting this issue as it might cause other potential harms in the future. I am asking for a formal acknowledgement and reasonable compesations for this case. A voucher equivient to single purchase would be fine to me. In addition, to avoid same accident occurs in the furture, I highly recommand a safety awareness course for every employee in this shop.

Your faithfully,
Connie Lu
