# G010 ‚ÄĒ Double Conjunction: `Although` and `However` Cannot Coexist

## Core Rule

`Although` and `However` are both concession markers. **Only one can be used per sentence.**

- `Although` = subordinating conjunction ‚Üí joins two clauses in ONE sentence
- `However` = adverb ‚Üí starts a NEW sentence after a period

They cannot appear in the same sentence. This is a direct Chinese grammar transfer (ťõĖÁĄ∂‚Ä¶šĹÜśėĮ‚Ä¶).

## Why it matters in IELTS

This error is **immediately visible** to examiners and signals Chinese grammar interference. It affects GRA in Writing. Count in personal error log: **4+ occurrences** ‚ÄĒ treat as highest priority.

## Common mistakes

‚ĚĆ `Although education is important, however, it alone cannot solve the problem.`
‚ĚĆ `Although prices have risen, however, people still buy.`
‚ĚĆ `Although X, but Y` (same logic ‚ÄĒ `although` + `but` is also wrong)

## Good examples

Choose ONE connector ‚ÄĒ not both:

‚úÖ `Although education is important, it alone cannot solve the problem.`
‚úÖ `Education is important. However, it alone cannot solve the problem.`
‚úÖ `While education is important, policy changes are also needed.`

## Reusable patterns

**Pick one. Use it. Finish the sentence.**

| Pattern | Template |
|---|---|
| `Although` | `Although [conceded point], [main claim].` |
| `However` | `[First claim]. However, [contrasting claim].` |
| `While` | `While [conceded point], [main claim].` |
| `Despite` | `Despite [noun phrase], [main claim].` |

**`Despite` reminder:** takes a noun phrase, not a clause.
‚úÖ `Despite the high cost, many people still buy.`
‚ĚĆ `Despite the cost is high, many people still buy.`

## Mini practice

Fix the double conjunction:

1. `Although the government has invested in education, however the results are disappointing.`
2. `Although fast fashion is cheap, however it creates environmental problems.`
3. `Although technology connects people, however it also isolates them.`

**Answers:**
1. Although the government has invested in education, the results are disappointing. / The government has invested in education. However, the results are disappointing.
2. Although fast fashion is cheap, it creates environmental problems.
3. Although technology connects people, it also isolates them.
