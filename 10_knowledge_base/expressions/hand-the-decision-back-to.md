# Expression

`hand the decision back to [someone]`

### Function

「把決定權交還給某人」——談 autonomy／empowerment 論點的自然說法。

### Usage

「交還」的動詞是 **hand back / give back**，不是 turn back（turn back = 往回走、
翻回去）。⚠️ 修正自 2026-07-13 原稿：`turn decisions back to them` ❌（「轉回去」
中文直譯）。近義：`give them the freedom to choose` / `let them decide for themselves`。

### Common Collocations

- hand the decision back to students / parents / local communities
- hand back control / responsibility

### Example

Allowing students to study whatever they like hands the decision back to them.

### IELTS Topics

- Education（自主選科）· Government（下放決策）· Work（授權員工）
