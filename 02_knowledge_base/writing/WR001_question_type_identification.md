# WR001 — Task 2 Question Type Identification

## Core Rule

**Always read the final instruction sentence first — not the topic.**

The final sentence tells you WHAT TO DO. The topic tells you what to write about. These are separate.

Classifying incorrectly causes Task Response failure — the most critical writing criterion.

## The Four Question Types

| Instruction keywords | Question type | What you must do |
|---|---|---|
| `Do you agree?` / `To what extent do you agree?` / `Is it a positive or negative development?` | **Agree / Disagree** | Choose a clear side. State it in intro. |
| `Discuss both views and give your own opinion` | **Discuss Both Views + Opinion** | Discuss BOTH fairly. Give YOUR view. |
| `What are the causes/reasons?` + `What solutions/measures?` OR `How can...?` | **Problem + Solution** | Give reasons AND solutions. Cover both. |
| Two separate questions with different focuses | **Two-Part Question** | Answer BOTH questions with equal depth. |

## Critical trap: Agree/Disagree vs Discuss Both

**The most common misclassification:**

`Is it a positive or negative development?` → This is **Agree/Disagree** (you choose one side)
`Discuss both views` → explicitly stated in the question

> If the question has two sides but asks for YOUR view → Agree/Disagree
> Only "discuss both" when those exact words appear

## Decision rule

Classify by what the instruction asks you to DO — not by the topic content.

**Step 1:** Cover the topic sentence.
**Step 2:** Read only the instruction.
**Step 3:** Match to the four types above.

## Common mistakes

❌ Seeing "some people think / others believe" and assuming → Discuss Both Views (wrong — check the instruction)
❌ Answering the question as Opinion when it's a Two-Part Question
❌ Only answering one part of a Problem+Solution question

## Reusable patterns

**Reading the question card:**
1. Read the final sentence (instruction)
2. Identify: `agree/disagree` | `discuss both` | `reasons + solutions` | `two questions`
3. Write the question type at the top of your planning paper
4. Then read the topic

## Mini practice

Classify each:

1. `Many people believe that university education should be free. Do you agree or disagree?`
2. `Some say technology brings people together. Others say it isolates us. Discuss both views and give your opinion.`
3. `Why do young people move to cities? What problems can this cause?`
4. `What are the causes of obesity? How can it be reduced?`

**Answers:**
1. Agree/Disagree
2. Discuss Both Views + Opinion
3. Two-Part Question (Why + What problems — two different questions)
4. Problem + Solution (causes + solutions — same issue)
