- 三個 bullet 應各自一段，目前擠在一起，段落分配不清楚。
- 第二段（介紹農曆新年和國際新年的差別）是**不必要的背景資訊**：
  - 題目問的是「對你國家的人有多重要、怎麼慶祝、你喜歡什麼」，這段並沒有回答任何一個 bullet。
  - 老師明確說：不回答題目的資訊不是中性，是**負分**（浪費字數 + 增加出錯機會）。
- 第三段只說「最重要的假期」，但沒解釋**為什麼**重要。老師說要往下想一層：為什麼是最重要的？

### 段落配置建議（老師說的）
- 慶祝方式那段太長、資訊太密；老師建議把其中一些內容移到「你喜歡什麼」那段，讓每段資訊量更平均。
- 「你喜歡什麼」這段目前只有一句話，要擴充。

### 語氣與人際感
- 信的語氣不夠自然——老師說「It looks like somebody you know, but not somebody you know that well.」
- 要想像真實的那個朋友（例如 Eric）：為什麼你知道他在做這個報告？你們是怎麼認識的？加這些細節讓信有溫度。
- 老師建議開頭可以像：「I heard you decided to do your college project about New Year in Taiwan...」

### 結尾
- 老師說給朋友的信不需要 "Yours sincerely"，只要名字就好，也可以加：
  `Love` / `Take care` / `All the best` / `Hope to catch up soon`
- "Regards" 絕對不適合用在給好朋友的信裡。

### 語言修正（老師指出）
- `I seen many people` → `I see many people`（一般現象用現在式，不是 seen）
- `would have arranged a worldwide trip` → 太正式；老師建議：`arrange a trip abroad` 或 `travel abroad`（自然、口語）

## 題目

Your English-speaking friend has asked for your help with a college project about celebrating New Year in different countries.

Write a letter to your friend. In your letter:
- say how important New Year is to people in your country
- describe how New Year is celebrated in your country
- explain what you like about New Year celebrations in your country

## 我的草稿

Dear Eric,

How have you been up to? I am glad to help with your school project. Here are some facts about New Year celebration in Taiwan.

In our culture, we celebrate both lunar new year and global new year. While lunar new year are more like traditional chinese new year, global new year is quite similar with western way.

Lunar new year can be seen as the most important holiday in our country. There is usually 7 to 9 days holidays. Almost all services will close during that time, including public service and private firms, excepts some emergy services.

Most families seen new year as a yearly family reunion as most members are free at the same time. Nowadays, I seen many people would have arranged a worldwide trip or simiply nearby outdoor activities. Among the holidays time, we definitely have a gathering family meal at the dinner time with some fun games like lucky words with red envelop, majia (a game similar to poker) or simply a lottery game. We believe that make us wellness and wealthy all the year.

I'm always looking forward to it, because we can see each other and have some memorable moments together.

Hope next time you could join us and experience yourself.

Connie

## Error Log 逐項對照

### 1. 單複數 / 主謂一致

| 原句 | 問題 | 修正 |
|---|---|---|
| `lunar new year are more like` | Lunar New Year 是單數主語 | `Lunar New Year is more like` |
| `There is usually 7 to 9 days holidays` | days 是複數，應用 are | `There are usually 7 to 9 days of holiday` |

### 2. 動詞形式 / 時態

| 原句 | 問題 | 修正 |
|---|---|---|
| `Most families seen new year` | seen 是過去分詞，不能當動詞 | `Most families see New Year` |
| `I seen many people` | 同上，且漏 have | `I have seen many people` |
| `would have arranged a worldwide trip` | 時態混亂（不是假設語氣情境） | `have arranged a trip abroad` 或 `plan a trip abroad` |

### 3. 介系詞

| 原句 | 問題 | 修正 |
|---|---|---|
| `quite similar with western way` | similar 後接 to，不接 with | `similar to the Western way` |
| `Among the holidays time` | among 用於三者以上個別成員之間，這裡應用 during | `During the holiday` / `During this time` |
| `experience yourself` | 少了受詞 | `experience it yourself` |

### 4. 其他文法 / 用字

| 原句 | 問題 | 修正 |
|---|---|---|
| `How have you been up to?` | 混淆了兩個說法 | `How have you been?` 或 `What have you been up to?`（選一個） |
| `excepts some emergy services` | excepts 不是正確用法；拼字錯 | `except for some emergency services` |
| `We believe that make us wellness and wealthy` | 結構錯誤（that 後需要主語；wellness/wealthy 詞性不一致） | `We believe this brings us health and good fortune` |

## 具體修改建議（結構優先）

**最重要的一件事：把三個 bullet 各自拆成一段。**

參考改法：

```
Dear Eric,

How have you been? I am happy to help with your college project.
Here is some information about New Year celebrations in Taiwan.

New Year is one of the most important events in our culture.
In fact, Taiwan celebrates two versions of it — Lunar New Year
(the traditional Chinese one) and the Gregorian New Year on
1 January. Lunar New Year is by far the bigger occasion,
with a public holiday of 7 to 9 days. During this time, almost
all businesses and public services close, except for emergency services.

The holiday is centred around family reunion. Most families
gather for a large dinner on New Year's Eve and enjoy traditional
activities such as giving red envelopes, playing mahjong, or
taking part in a lottery. Many people also use the long holiday
to travel, either within the country or abroad.

Personally, what I love most is the chance to see relatives I
rarely meet during the rest of the year. The atmosphere feels
warm and joyful, and there is always something fun going on.
I hope you can join us one day and experience it yourself!

Yours sincerely,
Connie Lu
```
