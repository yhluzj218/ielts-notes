# Sentence Pattern

`On the other hand, other people, including myself, believe that [your view]`

### Function

Body 2 topic sentence in a Discuss Both Views + Opinion essay: switches from the
weaker side (Body 1) to the side you support, and slips your own position in
naturally with `including myself` — no separate "I think" sentence needed.

### Structure

`On the other hand, other people, including myself, believe that [your view as a full clause].`

- `On the other hand` = 對比轉折，只能用在已經先講過另一邊之後（Body 1 → Body 2 的交界）
- `including myself` 前後都要逗號（插入語）
- ⚠️ 介系詞：`ON the other hand`，不是 `in another hand`（error_log P003 — 自己犯過）

### Usage

First sentence of Body 2, immediately after the Body 1 paragraph that fairly
explained the weaker side. After this sentence, continue the normal chain:
Explain → Example → Result. Source: Matt's discussion essay teaching, 2026-06-26
("And then the second paragraph you go to the other side and say, on the other
hand, other people, including myself, believe that...").

### Example

On the other hand, other people, including myself, believe that students learn far
more effectively when they are allowed to choose subjects they genuinely enjoy.

### IELTS Topics

- Any Discuss Both Views + Opinion question
