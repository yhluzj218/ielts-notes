# Chat Practice — 2026-06-25

**主題：** 颱風假 / 台灣颱風季

---

## 練習內容

Yeah, I've been doing pretty well. Actually, it's typhoon season in Taiwan at the moment, so the weather hasn't been great. The funny thing is that whenever a strong typhoon is approaching, we sometimes get a typhoon day off because it's considered too dangerous to go to work or school.

It feels a bit awkward to say this because typhoons can cause a lot of damage, but I think many people secretly hope for a day off. Fortunately, nothing serious happened where I live, so it's been quite a nice week for me.

---

## 語言觀察

✅ 這段沒有明顯語法錯誤，以下幾點特別自然：
- `it's typhoon season at the moment` — 時態用得對
- `secretly hope for a day off` — 道地說法
- `It feels a bit awkward to say this` — 語感很自然的讓步開頭
