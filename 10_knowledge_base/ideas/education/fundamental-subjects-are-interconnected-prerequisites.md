# Idea: Fundamental subjects are interconnected prerequisites

## Theme
education

## Learning Status
Practised

## Core Idea
數理等基礎學科的概念彼此相連，是多數其他學科的先修基礎；投資基礎學科帶來長期競爭優勢。

## Skeleton

| Step | Content |
|---|---|
| Claim | 基礎學科（science/technology/math）值得優先投資 |
| Reason | 這些學科的概念與其他知識領域高度相連 |
| Mechanism | 數學能力直接強化物理理解；基礎打好 → 後續學科學得更快（prerequisites for most other subjects） |
| Impact | 長期的競爭優勢（the greater the competitive advantage in the long term） |
| Example | a strong grasp of mathematics genuinely helps you comprehend physics |

## Reusable IELTS Angle
- 教育選科題 — 當「useful subjects」方的論點（本篇作為 Discussion Body 1 弱方使用）
- STEM 教育政策題（government should fund science education）
- 兒童教育題（what children should learn first）

## Speaking Use
- Part 3 教育類："Which subjects are most important at school?" — math→physics 的相連論證

## Useful Chunks
- a solid knowledge of fundamental subjects is essential to one's later studies
- the concepts themselves are closely connected to many other fields of knowledge
- prerequisites for most other subjects
