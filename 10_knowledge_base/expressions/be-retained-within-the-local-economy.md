## Expression

be retained within the local economy

### Function

Describe money or profit that remains in a community and continues to benefit local
businesses and residents.

### Usage

Use this expression when evaluating whether tourism, foreign investment, or economic
development creates genuine local benefits. It is formal and most useful in IELTS
Writing or Speaking Part 3.

`Within the local economy` emphasises where the money remains. It is generally more
natural here than `retained by the local economy`.

### Common Collocations

- revenue retained within the local economy
- profits retained within the local economy
- ensure that money is retained locally
- keep tourist spending within the local economy

### Example

Governments can support independent businesses to ensure that more tourism revenue
is retained within the local economy.
