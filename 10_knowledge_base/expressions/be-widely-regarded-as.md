# Expression

`be widely regarded as [X]`

### Function

「被普遍視為」——引述社會主流看法而不代表自己表態。Intro 改寫題目立場、或 Body 1
陳述他方觀點時的中性起手式，比 `people think` 精準自然。

### Usage

寫作 intro 與 Discussion Body 1 最常用；被動結構，主詞是被評價的事物。
表態留給 thesis，這個片語本身不站邊。

### Common Collocations

- widely regarded as "useful" / essential / one of the best
- is widely regarded as the most effective way to...

### Example

Some subjects, such as science and technology, are widely regarded as "useful".

### IELTS Topics

- 任何需要先陳述主流觀點的 Task 2（education / technology / government）
