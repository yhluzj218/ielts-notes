## 題目

You recently attended a training course for your work. Your employer has asked you for your feedback on the training course.

Write a letter to your employer. In your letter:
- remind your employer what the course was about
- explain why the course was useful to you in your work
- suggest why the course may not be suitable for some of your other colleagues

---

## 我的草稿（v2 — 2026-06-19 修訂）

Dear Will,

I am writing a short feeback about the class I have participated in the Mcdonald's customer success official training last weekend. The course is designed for employee with 2 years above. There is serveral reviews I would like to share with you for your reference.

The class was well-organised in a structured way, which indeed made it easily to linked back with the work. The best part is that we been seperated in different group in order to play different roles that actually frequently face in our daily worktime. That session was realistic and I have gained some new handle skills from others.

Although it was quite useful to me as a customer assistant, however for those whom are managers, seems a bit irrelevant to them. I believe that there has other more valuable class for them.

I am glad to share more if you like.

Regards,

Connie

## 切題與結構分析

### ⚠️ 第一段仍有新問題

`"I am writing a short feeback about the class I have participated in the Mcdonald's customer success official training last weekend."`

句子結構斷掉——"about the class" 之後又接了另一個 "I have participated in...training"，兩個從句疊在一起。

建議：
```
I am writing to share my feedback on the McDonald's customer success training I attended last weekend.
```

同段其他問題：
- `"feeback"` → `"feedback"`
- `"designed for employee"` → `"designed for employees with over 2 years of experience"`
- `"There is several reviews"` → `"There are several points"` / `"I have a few thoughts"`（主謂一致）

---

### ⚠️ Bullet 3 仍發展不足

第三段只說「對經理來說不太相關，應該有更有價值的課程」，沒有解釋**為什麼**不適合。應補一句理由，例如課程內容是針對前線客服操作，而非管理層職責。

## Error Log 逐項對照（v2 剩餘問題）

### 🔴 時態錯誤（第二段，未修）

- `"we been seperated"` → `"we were separated"`
- `"I have gained"` → 配合 last weekend，用 `"I gained"` 更一致

### 🔴 Although + however 雙重連接詞（未修）

`"Although it was quite useful to me..., however for those who are managers..."`

只能留一個：
- `Although it was useful to me, it may not be ideal for managers.`
- `It was useful to me. However, for managers, it may not be relevant.`

### 🔴 單複數（未修）

- `"different group"` → `"different groups"`

### ⚠️ 正式結尾（部分修好）

加了全名 Connie ✅，但 `"Regards"` → 建議改為 `"Yours sincerely"`。
【筆記外補充】Dear + 名字 → Yours sincerely；Dear Sir/Madam → Yours faithfully。

## 具體文法修改建議（v2 剩餘）

| 原句 | 問題 | 修改 |
|------|------|------|
| `I am writing a short feeback about the class I have participated in the...training` | 句子結構斷 + 拼字 | `I am writing to share my feedback on the...training I attended last weekend.` |
| `designed for employee with 2 years above` | 複數 + 介系詞 | `designed for employees with over 2 years of experience` |
| `There is several reviews` | 主謂一致 | `There are several points` |

| `for those whom are managers` | who vs whom | `for those who are managers` |
| `there has other more valuable class` | 文法 | `there are other more valuable courses` |
