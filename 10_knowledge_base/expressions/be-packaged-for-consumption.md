## Expression

be packaged for [audience] consumption

### Function

Describe how culture, information, or entertainment is deliberately simplified,
adapted, and presented as a product for a particular audience.

### Usage

Use this expression when criticising commercialisation or the loss of authenticity.
`Packaged` is metaphorical: the subject is reshaped to become easier to sell or
consume, rather than literally placed in packaging.

The expression is formal and particularly useful in IELTS Writing. Name the intended
audience before `consumption`, such as `tourist`, `public`, or `mass`.

### Common Collocations

- be packaged for tourist consumption
- be packaged for mass consumption
- be packaged for public consumption
- traditions packaged as entertainment

### Example

Religious ceremonies may lose their original meaning when they are packaged for
tourist consumption.
