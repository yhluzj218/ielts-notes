# 批改評語 — Train Refund Letter

**日期：** 2026-06-19
**Task 類型：** Task 1（正式投訴信，formal letter）

---

## 題目

You recently bought some train tickets for a journey a week in advance. When you went to the station to catch the train, you were told you could not use the tickets and the staff were very unhelpful to you.

Write a letter to the train company. In your letter:
- describe the problem you had with the tickets
- say why you were unhappy with the staff
- suggest what action the train company should take

---

## 你的草稿

> Dear Sir or Madam,
>
> I'm writing this letter for a refund of the train tickets I have purchased last week. As a loyality customer, while I was planing for the family trip, I bought the tickets on the official websites and have the e-tickets stored in my mobile app. While I was just about entered the gates, one staff told me I was not allowed using that ticket, without provings any further assistance. It ended up with unhappy experience for me and my plan was delayed as a result of it. Therefore, I am requesting for a refunds of tickets as well as $50 compensation for the loss caused by this. I believe the staff with bad attitude is just a special case. However, it lower the trusts. I am hoping this wont happened in the future.

## 一、切題與結構（最先看）

**結論：三個 bullet 都有碰到，但全擠在同一段，結構是最大失分點。**

- 題目要求三個 bullet：①票的問題 ②對工作人員不滿 ③建議行動
- 你的信只有**一整段**，三點都混在一起，沒有分段
- 正確格式應是：

```
Para 1（開頭）  → 說明來信原因（1–2 句）
Para 2          → Bullet 1：票的問題（描述具體情況）
Para 3          → Bullet 2：對工作人員不滿（說明為何不滿）
Para 4          → Bullet 3：你的要求／建議行動
結尾            → Yours faithfully, [全名]
```

- **沒有 Yours faithfully + 全名** — 以 Dear Sir or Madam 開頭就必須這樣收尾，這是 error_log 明確標記的失分點。

---

## 二、error_log 逐項對照

### 🔴 時態錯誤（error_log 第 1 條）
```
❌ I have purchased last week
✅ I purchased last week
```
"last week" 是明確過去時間 → 必用過去式，不能用現在完成式。這是你的高頻錯誤，這次又犯了。

---

### 🔴 不可數名詞加 s（error_log 第 2 條）
```
❌ one staff（staff 是集合不可數名詞，不能說 one staff）
✅ a staff member

❌ it lower the trusts
✅ it lowers trust（trust 在此是不可數）
```

---

### 🔴 單複數（error_log 第 5 條）
```
❌ a refunds of tickets
✅ a refund for the tickets
```
"a" 後面不能加複數 s。這也是 error_log 裡點名的例子。

---

### 其他文法問題

```
❌ not allowed using
✅ not allowed to use

❌ without provings any further assistance
✅ without providing any further assistance
（provings 不是英文字）

❌ It ended up with unhappy experience
✅ It resulted in an unpleasant experience

❌ requesting for a refund
✅ requesting a refund
（request 不接 for）

❌ it lower the trusts
✅ it lowers trust
（主詞三單 it → 動詞加 s）

❌ this wont happened
✅ this won't happen
（won't 拼錯；happen 不能用過去式，這是對未來的期望）
```

---

### 拼字（次要，但影響 Lexical Resource）
```
loyality → loyalty
planing  → planning
```

---

## 三、具體修改建議

**最重要的兩件事（這次練習的重點）：**

1. **分三段寫三個 bullet**，每段專注一個點，不要全塞在一起。
2. **加上正式結尾**：`Yours faithfully,` 換行後寫全名。

**示範重寫架構（你來填內容）：**

```
Dear Sir or Madam,

[Para 1] I am writing to complain about an issue I experienced
with train tickets I purchased last week.

[Para 2 — Bullet 1: 票的問題]
I bought the tickets on the official website in advance and
stored them in my mobile app. However, when I arrived at
the station, I was told by a staff member that I could not
use them. No explanation was given.

[Para 3 — Bullet 2: 對工作人員不滿]
The staff member who refused my entry was unhelpful and
dismissive. He/She did not offer any assistance or direct
me to someone who could resolve the issue, which left me
feeling frustrated and confused.

[Para 4 — Bullet 3: 建議行動]
I would like to request a full refund for the tickets, as
well as compensation of $50 for the inconvenience caused.
I also suggest that staff receive better training in
customer service.

Yours faithfully,
[Full Name]
```

---

## 四、優點（別丟掉）

- 開頭 "Dear Sir or Madam," 正確。
- 詞彙選用整體流暢（compensation、assistance、dismissive 方向都對）。
- 三個 bullet 都有涵蓋，沒有漏掉任何一點。

---

## 總結

這封信的問題不是英文不好，是**格式結構**。三點擠一段 + 沒有 Yours faithfully，是 Task 1 信件最基本的失分點。時態（last week + have purchased）也是你的慣性錯誤，要有意識地在下筆前提醒自己。把結構修好，這篇可以到 6+。
