## [Structure] 句型 / 連接詞

---

**Front:** One of the most memorable...

**Back:** One of the most memorable trips I've ever taken was to Kyoto — everything about it felt completely different from my everyday life.

---

**Front:** I'm the kind of person who...

**Back:** I'm the kind of person who needs a bit of time to warm up in new situations, but once I do, I'm quite comfortable talking to anyone.

---

**Front:** One of the biggest milestones in my life...

**Back:** One of the biggest milestones in my life was moving to Taipei on my own — it was the first time I had to figure everything out by myself.

---

**Front:** It has completely changed the way I...

**Back:** It has completely changed the way I think about work — I used to believe being busy meant being productive, but now I focus on what actually matters.

---

## [Expression] 自然搭配 / 短語

---

**Front:** Quite a few...

**Back:** Quite a few of my friends have moved abroad in the past few years, so we mostly keep in touch online now.
