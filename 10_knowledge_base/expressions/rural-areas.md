## Expression

rural areas

### Function

Refer to places in the countryside rather than towns or cities.

### Usage

Use this neutral noun phrase when discussing people, services, infrastructure, or
development outside urban areas. `Rural` is an adjective, so it normally appears
before a noun such as `areas`, `communities`, `roads`, or `households`.

Do not use `rural` by itself as a plural noun for places. Say `rural areas` or name
the specific rural place.

### Common Collocations

- people living in rural areas
- access to services in rural areas
- development in rural areas
- remote rural areas
- urban and rural areas

### Example

Governments can improve access to healthcare by expanding transport services in
rural areas.
