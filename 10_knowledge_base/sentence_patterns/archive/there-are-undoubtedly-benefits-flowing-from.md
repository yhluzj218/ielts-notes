## Sentence Pattern

There are undoubtedly benefits flowing from [development or trend].

### Function

Introduce the positive effects of a development before explaining or evaluating
them.

### Structure

`There are undoubtedly benefits flowing from + noun phrase.`

### Usage

Use this pattern as a formal topic sentence in an advantages–disadvantages discussion
or when conceding the positive side of a trend. The noun phrase should name a
development that produces multiple benefits.

`Flowing from` is formal and more suitable for IELTS Writing than casual speech.
Follow the sentence with specific benefits rather than leaving the claim unsupported.

### Example

There are undoubtedly benefits flowing from the widespread adoption of remote work.

### IELTS Topics

- Technology
- Work and employment
- Education
- Tourism
- Transport
- Globalisation
