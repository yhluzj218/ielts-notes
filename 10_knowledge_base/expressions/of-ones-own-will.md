# Expression

`of one's own will`

### Function

「出於自己的意願」——描述自主、非被迫的行為。談 autonomy／motivation 論點時的
固定搭配。

### Usage

放在動詞後：`learn / choose / participate + of their own will`。
⚠️ 修正自 2026-07-13 原稿錯誤：`under her/his willing` ❌（willing 是形容詞，
名詞是 will；介系詞是 of 不是 under）。近義：`willingly`、`voluntarily`、
`of their own accord`。

### Common Collocations

- learn of their own will
- do something of one's own free will（加 free 語氣更強）

### Example

Students tend to perform better when they are learning of their own will.

### IELTS Topics

- Education（自主學習）· Work（工作動機）· Government（自願 vs 強制政策）
