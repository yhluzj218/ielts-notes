# Vocabulary — excavate

## Front

To remove earth, recover something by digging, or hollow out an area.

## Back

### Word

### Pronunciation

### Part of Speech

verb

### Frequency

Approximately once every 600 pages.

### Examples

- **Business:** Once researchers could easily search through a century of paper,
  they could excavate gems that were buried in the corporate archives—like
  Moylan’s original memo.
- **Science:** We want to save it for the next generation, with better technology
  and methodology to excavate at that depth.
