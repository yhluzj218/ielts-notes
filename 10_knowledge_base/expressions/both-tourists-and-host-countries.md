## Expression

both tourists and host countries

### Function

Refer concisely to the two main groups affected by international tourism: visitors
and the countries that receive them.

### Usage

Use this expression when discussing benefits, costs, or responsibilities shared by
both sides of international tourism.

Use `for` when describing advantages or benefits, `to` after adjectives such as
`beneficial`, and `on` when describing effects or impacts.

### Common Collocations

- benefits for both tourists and host countries
- advantages for both tourists and host countries
- be beneficial to both tourists and host countries
- effects on both tourists and host countries
- responsibilities shared by both tourists and host countries

### Example

Sustainable tourism can create long-term benefits for both tourists and host
countries.
