# NotebookLM Generation Prompt

Please use the source briefing article as your main source.

Create a two-host English podcast discussion for an IELTS learner.

The goal is to help me broaden my ideas and understand different viewpoints on this
IELTS topic.

Please follow these requirements:

1. Start by briefly introducing the IELTS question and the question type.
2. Explain the core issue in simple but natural English.
3. Have two hosts discuss at least three viewpoints from the source article.
4. Include natural disagreement, clarification, and comparison.
5. Discuss both benefits and drawbacks if the question requires it.
6. If solutions or responses are included in the source, discuss which ones are realistic.
7. Use natural but clear English suitable for an IELTS learner around Listening Band 7.
8. Avoid unnecessary small talk.
9. Avoid motivational language.
10. Do not add unsupported facts beyond the source article.
11. Do not turn the discussion into a formal essay.
12. At the end, provide a clear recap of:
    - the main benefit
    - the main drawback
    - one important example
    - the final position
    - two or three useful IELTS expressions

Please make the discussion useful for IELTS Writing Task 2 and Speaking Part 3.

## Validation Checklist

- The user supplied the topic or material that unambiguously identifies it.
- At least three viewpoints are traceable to allowed sources.
- Background contains no unsupported facts.
- Every example or support item is source-grounded or explicitly marked absent.
- Every viewpoint identifies its Source basis and includes a Natural IELTS sentence.
- Solutions appear only when relevant and include what, why, and limitation.
- The Desired Position is used only when supported.
- Source Log appears only in Research-Assisted Mode.
- The NotebookLM prompt prohibits unsupported facts.
- No full Podcast or output-practice content is present.
