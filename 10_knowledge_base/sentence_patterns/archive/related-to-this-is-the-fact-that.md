## Sentence Pattern

Related to this is the fact that [clause].

### Function

Introduce an additional point that is closely connected to the argument immediately
before it.

### Structure

`Related to this is the fact that + clause.`

### Usage

Use this pattern to extend an argument with a distinct but logically connected point.
The connection should be obvious; do not use it merely as a decorative linking
phrase.

It is formal and better suited to IELTS Writing than everyday speech. Because the
structure is relatively wordy, use it sparingly.

### Example

Related to this is the fact that many temporary workers receive no paid leave.

### IELTS Topics

- Work and employment
- Tourism
- Education
- Technology
- Environment
- Government policy
