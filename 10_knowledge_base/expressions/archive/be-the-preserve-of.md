## Expression

be the preserve of

### Function

Describe an activity, opportunity, or resource that is available almost exclusively
to a particular group.

### Usage

Use this expression to discuss inequality, exclusivity, or historical changes in
access. It is formal and especially useful in IELTS Writing and more developed
Speaking Part 3 answers.

The expression is usually followed by a privileged or specialised group.

### Common Collocations

- be the preserve of the wealthy
- be the preserve of the elite
- be the preserve of specialists
- no longer be the preserve of a privileged few

### Example

Higher education is no longer the preserve of a small social elite.

### IELTS Topics

- Education
- Technology
- Tourism
- Culture
- Social inequality
- Health
