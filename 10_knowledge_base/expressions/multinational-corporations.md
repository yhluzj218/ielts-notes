## Expression

multinational corporations

### Function

Refer to large companies that own or control business operations in more than one
country.

### Usage

Use this formal term when discussing global business, foreign investment, taxation,
employment, or the movement of profits between countries.

`Multinational companies` is a slightly less formal alternative. Avoid using the
term as an automatic criticism; explain the specific benefit or problem created by
the corporations.

### Common Collocations

- profits taken out by multinational corporations
- multinational corporations operating overseas
- attract multinational corporations
- regulate multinational corporations
- tax multinational corporations
- employment created by multinational corporations

### Example

Developing countries may attract multinational corporations without receiving a
fair share of the profits they generate.
