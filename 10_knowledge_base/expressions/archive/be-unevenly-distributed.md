## Expression

be unevenly distributed

### Function

Describe money, resources, opportunities, or benefits that are not shared equally
across people, groups, or regions.

### Usage

Use this passive expression when discussing inequality or showing that an apparent
overall benefit does not reach everyone. It is neutral to formal and works well in
IELTS Writing and Speaking Part 3.

Name what is distributed and then explain which groups receive more or less.

### Common Collocations

- income is unevenly distributed
- wealth is unevenly distributed
- benefits are unevenly distributed
- resources are unevenly distributed
- opportunities are unevenly distributed

### Example

The economic benefits of urban development are often unevenly distributed.

### IELTS Topics

- Social inequality
- Economic development
- Tourism
- Government policy
- Education
- Healthcare
