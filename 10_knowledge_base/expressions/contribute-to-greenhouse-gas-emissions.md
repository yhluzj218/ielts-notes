## Expression

contribute to greenhouse gas emissions

### Function

Explain that an activity is one of several causes of emissions that drive climate
change.

### Usage

Use this expression when an activity increases emissions but is not their only
cause. `Contribute to` presents a measured causal claim and is usually more accurate
than saying that one activity directly causes climate change.

Use `greenhouse gas emissions`, not simply `greenhouse gases`, when referring to the
amount released by transport, industry, or consumption.

### Common Collocations

- contribute significantly to greenhouse gas emissions
- transport-related greenhouse gas emissions
- reduce greenhouse gas emissions
- rising greenhouse gas emissions

### Example

Frequent air travel contributes significantly to global greenhouse gas emissions.
