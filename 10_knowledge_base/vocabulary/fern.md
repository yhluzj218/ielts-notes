# Vocabulary — fern

## Front

A leafy green plant with no flowers or seeds that reproduces by spores and typically grows in shady, damp spots.

## Back

### Word

**fern**

### Pronunciation

US /fərn/, UK /fən/

### Part of Speech

Noun

### Frequency

Not available from the consulted source.

### Examples

- **Environment:** The forest floor was covered in ferns, thriving in the deep shade beneath the canopy.
- **Home & Lifestyle:** She kept a fern by the window because it tolerated low light better than most houseplants.

### Other Forms

- ferns (plural)
