# University Tuition Fees — Agree or Disagree

- **日期：** 2026-06-19
- **類型：** Task 2（Opinion — To what extent do you agree or disagree?）

## 題目

University students should pay all the costs associated with their education because having a degree benefits them individually more than society as a whole. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

## 我的草稿（v2 — 2026-06-19 修訂）

Some people suggested that pupils in university are supposed to be charged with the full tuition fees due to a college certificate benefit them more than society as a whole. I firmly oppose this statement for two main reasons.

First and foremost, paying all fees might be an unaffordable burden for some families. Therefore, they might ended up go for work rather than continue their study. However, retaining those students in schools will have a long-term benefits in general. For example, some enterpereurs founded companies while they are at school times, which in return it have profound advantages for society, such as more job opportunites or GPD contributions.

In addition, fully charged educational costs might escalate the gaps between different levels of families in a result of wealthy families tends to have more choices compared with normal families. For instance, if students have to pay all tuition fees, the university like Cambridge might become a school that full of wealthy people because of extremly costs.

It is true that people should be responsible for what they gain. However, in my perspective, it think education itself should not be treated as a merchandise, whereas it somehow is social resibililbilty which is a basic right as a citizens. We pay taxes to government to create a better quality living environment and therefore government should also afford a part of portion of tuition fees.

To sum up, I strongly disagree that all fees should be paid by students. By the reasons above, I believe government should share a portion of it to make a profound benefits as well as social responsibilties.
