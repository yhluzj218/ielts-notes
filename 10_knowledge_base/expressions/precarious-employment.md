## Expression

precarious employment

### Function

Describe work that is insecure, unstable, or likely to end with little warning.

### Usage

Use this expression when evaluating job quality rather than merely counting how many
jobs an industry creates. It is formal and particularly useful in IELTS Writing and
Speaking Part 3.

`Precarious` focuses on insecurity. If the main problem is low pay or poor working
conditions, state those problems separately.

### Common Collocations

- precarious employment
- precarious work
- precarious jobs
- precarious working conditions
- seasonal and precarious employment

### Example

Although the tourism sector creates jobs, many local workers remain trapped in
precarious employment.
