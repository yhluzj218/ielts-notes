# What Kind of Place?

**Type:** Vocabulary chant
**Target pattern:** "It's a _______ place"
**Topic:** Describing places (IELTS Speaking Part 2)
**Date:** 2026-07-01

---

## Lyrics

**[Verse 1]**
Dilapidated, falling down
The oldest ruin in the town
Dilapidated, walls are grey
Nobody fixed it anyway

**[Chorus]**
It's a dilapidated place
It's a dilapidated place
Say it once, say it twice
Dilapidated — isn't that nice?

**[Verse 2]**
Historic, full of stories old
Historic, tales have all been told
Walk the streets and feel the past
Historic places always last

**[Chorus]**
It's a historic place
It's a historic place
Say it once, say it twice
Historic — isn't that nice?

**[Verse 3]**
Run-down, crumbling and worn
Run-down, shabby and torn
Nobody comes to fix it up
Run-down from the bottom up

**[Chorus]**
It's a run-down place
It's a run-down place
Say it once, say it twice
Run-down — isn't that nice?

**[Verse 4]**
Spacious, open, wide and bright
Spacious feels completely right
Lots of room and lots of air
Spacious, spacious everywhere

**[Chorus]**
It's a spacious place
It's a spacious place
Say it once, say it twice
Spacious — isn't that nice?

**[Verse 5]**
Modern, shiny, glass and steel
Modern, that's the city feel
Everything is clean and new
Modern buildings, modern view

**[Chorus]**
It's a modern place
It's a modern place
Say it once, say it twice
Modern — isn't that nice?

**[Ending — slow chant]**
Dilapidated...
Historic...
Run-down...
Spacious...
Modern...

It's a _______ place.
It's a _______ place.
Now you're ready — describe that space!
