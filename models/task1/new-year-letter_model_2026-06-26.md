## 範本全文

Dear Eric,

What a great topic for your project! I am happy to help — Taiwan actually has some fascinating New Year traditions I think you will love including.

New Year, particularly Lunar New Year, is easily the most significant holiday in Taiwan. It holds an importance that few other occasions can match — public offices, banks, schools and most private businesses all close for up to nine consecutive days, showing just how central it is to our way of life.

The celebrations revolve around family reunion. On New Year's Eve, families gather for a grand dinner, with dishes chosen for their symbolic meaning — fish for abundance and dumplings for wealth. The days that follow are filled with visiting relatives, exchanging red envelopes containing lucky money, and playing traditional games such as mahjong or joining local temple festivals.

What I personally love most about this time of year is the sense of warmth and belonging it creates. No matter how scattered family members are throughout the year, Lunar New Year pulls everyone home. The festive atmosphere — the sounds, the food, the laughter around the table — is something I look forward to every single year.

I hope this gives you some useful material for your project. Do let me know if you would like more details!

Take care,
Connie

### 預估 Band

**Band 7–7.5**

- Task Achievement 7：三個 bullet 各自一段，覆蓋完整，開頭加入個人連結讓信有真實感。
- Coherence & Cohesion 7：段落清楚，每段聚焦單一 bullet，連接自然不生硬。
- Lexical Resource 7：用字自然道地（revolve around、the days that follow、scattered），無堆砌。
- Grammar 7：句型有變化（em dash、participle clause、no matter how），錯誤少。

---

### 這篇好在哪（我要學的結構/手法）

- **開頭不是「I am writing to...」**：用 "What a great topic!" 直接帶入對話感，讓信一開始就像真人在說話。
- **每個 bullet 各自一段，且段落份量均衡**：三個 body paragraph 長度相近，不會某段只有一句。
- **Bullet 1 說「重要性」時加了具體證據**：不只說「最重要的假期」，用「九天公休、所有機關關閉」來支撐這個說法。
- **Bullet 2 加象徵意義**：菜餚的象徵（魚=豐收、餃子=財富）比直接列菜單更有文化深度，且字數效率高。
- **Bullet 3 從感受切入，不列清單**：用「歸屬感、節慶氣氛」代替再列一遍活動，避免跟 Bullet 2 重複。
- **結尾保持友善互動**：Do let me know if you would like more details — 讓信有來有往的感覺。

---

### 值得學的表達（自然、可借用）

- `What a great topic for your project!` — 開場讚美對方，自然帶入主題，適合任何幫朋友忙的信
- `I am happy to help` — 比 "I am glad to help" 更口語自然
- `is easily the most significant` — easily 加強語氣，比 "is the most important" 更有力
- `holds an importance that few other occasions can match` — 說某事重要的進階說法，不直接說「最重要」
- `revolve around` — 描述慶祝活動的核心是什麼（The celebrations revolve around family reunion）
- `the days that follow` — 指假期後續那幾天，比 "the following days" 更自然流暢
- `scattered` — 形容家人平時分散各地，一個字說清楚（no matter how scattered family members are）
- `pulls everyone home` — 描述節日把人拉回家，比 "makes everyone come back" 更有畫面
- `Do let me know` — 比 "Please let me know" 更友善、更口語（do 在這裡加強語氣）

### 連接詞 / 句型片語（可直接套用）

| 片語 | 位置/功能 |
|---|---|
| `What a great topic!` | 開頭 — 帶入主題，友善暖場 |
| `particularly [X]` | 段落開頭 — 縮小範圍，聚焦重點（New Year, particularly Lunar New Year） |
| `few other occasions can match` | 說明重要性 — 比較式強調，避免說「最...」 |
| `— [em dash] —` | 句中補充細節，保持句子流暢不加子句 |
| `The days that follow are filled with...` | 描述後續活動，時間感清楚 |
| `No matter how [adj] [subject] [verb], [result]` | 讓步 + 轉折 — 表達節日把人拉回來的力量 |
| `I hope this gives you some useful material` | 結尾 — 呼應對方的需求（寫報告），比「Bye」更有禮貌有溫度 |
| `Do let me know if you would like more details` | 結尾互動句 — 友善、開放對話 |
