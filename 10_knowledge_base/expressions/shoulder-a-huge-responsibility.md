## Expression

`shoulder a huge responsibility`

### Function

State that a person or group carries a heavy duty or obligation — more natural and
書面 than "have a big responsibility".

### Usage

Use in a concession sentence before disagreeing with a proposal, or whenever a topic
involves someone being responsible for an outcome (parents, governments, companies,
individuals).

### Common Collocations

- shoulder a huge/heavy/significant responsibility
- shoulder the burden of [X]
- shoulder the blame for [X]

### Example

It is true that parents shoulder a huge responsibility, but this does not mean the
state should dictate how they raise their children.
