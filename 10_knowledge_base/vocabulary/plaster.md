# Vocabulary — plaster

## Front

(noun) A mixture of lime or gypsum, sand, and water that hardens into a smooth solid used to coat walls and ceilings; in British English, also an adhesive bandage. (verb) To coat a surface with this material, or to cover something conspicuously.
