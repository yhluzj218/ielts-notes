# IELTS Advice: Daily progress

> Source: Simon (IELTS Simon)
> Saved: 2026-06-30

In my opinion, the way to achieve any goal in life is to take a small step in the right direction every day.
Notice that I said "take a small step". You shouldn't expect to make massive progress every day; a little progress is enough if you're improving daily.
This is why I like sharing short, daily lessons. My aim is to help you to learn something useful each day, even if it's just one new phrase in English.
Study task:
Today I'd like you to simply think about your progress, in terms of both IELTS preparation and your English.
Do you feel that you're taking a small step forward every day?
If you've followed my lessons over the last week, what have you learned? What can you remember? What did you note down?
Take a few minutes to think about these questions and to review your study progress. Feel free to share your thoughts or ask any questions in the comments area below.
