## 題目

Some people prefer to live in hot climates, whereas others love the lifestyle in countries with cold climates. Discuss both views and explain which climate you prefer.

## 我的草稿

In a century ago, you may not have a chance to take a look of the lifestyles living in a contrast weather condition. Nowadays, people are given the options to decide which places they are prefer to live in. While Some people have preference of hot areas, some people prefer to live in colder places. This essay argues that living in the former countries has benefits to explore biological diversity, and latter one is less bother of insects and unexpected disease spead out.

The most appealing reason that living in a hot weather is able to see the diverse plants and animals. A big amount of animals rely on a sufficient sunlight and humidity to provide abundant food supplies. Hot environment creates a great condition that fits many ecosystems. As the result of it, people are able to see and explore those amazing creatures. For example, diving lovers love to go to tropical islands for seeing the unique oceanic features like menta,sharks and coral rocks.

In contrast, living in a colder place might be a worried-free choice for people who hates annoying insects. On the other side of biology diversity, hot and humidity environment tends to grow backteria and unpleasing insects. Those insects are usually a common media to carry contagious diseases. For people who desperate want to escape from these annoying insects, living in a cold place would ease a lot of pressures to them. For example, dange fever is a known issue that happens every year in Asian and needs extra efforts make it in control. Hundreds of cockroaches lower the quality of life, which make the cold places more attractive. That is exact reason why I prefer to stay in cold-weather countries.

To sum up, it is true that a place with biology diversity is appealing. However, the ievitable insects is a considerable troublesom for insect hater, and gives a powerful push power for them to choose living in a cold countries.
