# Print vs Digital News

**日期：** 2026-06-19
**類型：** Task 2

## 題目

In the future, nobody will buy printed newspapers or books because they will be able to read everything they want online without paying.

To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement?

## 我的草稿

There is an inevitable trend happens around the world: printed newspapers or books are getting replaced by digital mediums, such as social media or online articles. The latter can be retrived with zero cost.
For my personal perspective, I have the positive attitude towards this trend. There are severals reasons I would like to deliberate in the followings statement.
Firstly, I think information itself should be treated as fundamental benefits. It could be fairly acquired inbetween different backgrounds without enonomic limits or physical limitations. As a result, it accelerates the exchange of knowledge and therefore most people can be benefits from this.
However, information expansion could also be considered as a negative effect. Since online information does not need to responsible for what it says. The information may contains in proper or not proven informations for those whom are not able to identify the accuracy of the content. For my own experience, my elder relatives prefer sharing the misleading health information in social medium. People whom received this information can easily forward without any proper content provements. The responsibility of the indirect bad effect or even a worse situation , is vague and no boby needs to pay for it.
Although the above disadvantage, my point of view is certainly agree with physical paper work should be gradually replaced by e-papers with free charge. We should think of how to enable reader to have ability to select the content which is trust-worty, instead of making it as a serivce by charging money.
