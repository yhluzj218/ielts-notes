# Question 1

**Describe a challenge you overcame.**

## Your Original Response

> Okay, I overcame a challenge when I climbed Mount Fuji.
>
> I like outdoor activities, but my physical ability is not that good. Although I keep exercising once a week, hiking Mount Fuji was still a huge challenge because it required around ten hours of hiking.
>
> It required not only physical strength but also muscular endurance. Besides that, I had to carry all my own gear during the hike.
>
> What kept me going was my goal. I had always wanted to see the view from the top of Mount Fuji at least once in my life.
>
> Before the trip, I made a training plan and increased my strength training. I also watched YouTube videos to prepare everything I needed, such as offline maps, proper clothing and equipment for extreme weather and unexpected situations.
>
> In the end, the hike turned out to be much easier than I had expected. I finished it much faster than I had imagined and took many amazing photos. It became one of the most unforgettable experiences in my life.

---

## Refined Version (Band 7.5–8)

> One of the biggest challenges I've ever overcome was climbing Mount Fuji.
>
> Although I really enjoy outdoor activities, I've never considered myself someone with great physical stamina. Even though I exercise regularly, hiking for nearly ten hours while carrying a heavy backpack was still a huge challenge for me.
>
> What motivated me most was a simple goal: I wanted to experience the sunrise and enjoy the breathtaking view from the summit at least once in my life.
>
> To prepare for the trip, I followed a structured training plan and focused on building both my strength and endurance. I also spent time watching YouTube videos and researching what equipment I would need, including offline maps, proper clothing, and ways to deal with extreme weather conditions.
>
> Looking back, the hike was actually much easier than I had expected because I had prepared so thoroughly. Reaching the summit gave me an incredible sense of achievement, and the photos I took there are still some of my favourites today.

---

## Corrections

### Grammar

❌ my physical ability not that good
✅ my physical stamina isn't very good

❌ muscles, persistence and strength
✅ strength, endurance and perseverance

❌ less than one-third time
✅ in much less time than I had expected

### Vocabulary Upgrade

| 原本說 | 更自然的說法 |
|--------|------------|
| physical ability | physical stamina |
| muscle endurance | endurance |
| face extreme weather | handle extreme weather conditions |
| gave me motivation | motivated me |
