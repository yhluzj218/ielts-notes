# Expression

`from this point of view`

### Function

「從這個觀點來看」——承接上一句陳述的立場，引出該立場下的推論。

### Usage

介系詞固定是 **from**：觀點是你「從哪裡看出去」的位置，不是壓在頭上的東西。
⚠️ 修正自 2026-07-13 原稿：`Under this point of view` ❌（「在觀點之下」中文直譯）。
近義：`from this perspective` / `seen from this angle`。

### Common Collocations

- from this point of view
- from a practical / an economic point of view
- from the point of view of [group]

### Example

From this point of view, students should focus on subjects that lead directly to employment.

### IELTS Topics

- 任何 Task 2 — Body 段內承接觀點做推論時
