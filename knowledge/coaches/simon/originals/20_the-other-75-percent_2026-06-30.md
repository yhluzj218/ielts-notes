# IELTS Advice: The other 75%

> Source: Simon (IELTS Simon)
> Saved: 2026-06-30

Remember that grammar is only 25% of your score, and it's easier to improve the other 75% (task response, coherence, vocabulary).
This 75% can be summed up in one word: ideas.
If you have good ideas, you can answer the question well (task response).
Present your ideas in a clear and logical way (coherence and cohesion).
Ideas are expressed as words and phrases (vocabulary).
A key aim in the lessons on this site is to work on these three areas. We won't neglect grammar – I often correct people's sentences in the comments area – but it isn't my main focus.
