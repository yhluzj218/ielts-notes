## 題目

Nowadays famous people are photographed by professional photographers everywhere they go. Some people say this is a good thing because the public are interested in their lives. Other people think that photographers are wrong to follow famous people. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.

## Joe 逐段評語

### Introduction — 比上次更自然，well done，但還可以再發展

- 「This is a more natural introduction. I like that. So keep developing that.」（比 company-size 那篇進步）
- **可以再發展**：Joe 給了一個示範，把開頭用「以前 vs 現在」的對比展開：

> "100 years ago, most celebrities were only known to the public through newspapers. But today, their faces are everywhere. They appear in social media, magazines, television, and billboard advertising. This has completely changed the dynamic between individuals and celebrities."

（呼應他 7/2 教的「then vs now」narrative opening——這次給了完整的示範版本。）

- **Intro 要把兩邊立場都寫清楚**：「Also, write what both sides of your opinion are. So, agree and disagree. You can write the agree and disagree side a bit more clearly.」opinion 本身已經 fairly clear，缺的是把「同意方 vs 反對方」兩邊更明確地預告出來。
- **不要在 introduction 寫 "I"**：「Written in academic style, mostly apart from you wrote I in the introduction.」——這是 LR 被扣到 6 的原因之一（我的草稿：`before I presenting my opionion`）。⚠️ 這是 Joe 第二次講第三人稱表態（7/11 company-size：「Write your opinion in the third person」）。

### Body 1（Michael Jackson）— 好，但舉例前要先解釋更多

- 「Explain a bit more before giving an example.」先把道理講完整，再進例子。Joe 的示範：

> "Often fans only see one very small part of their celebrity's personality, but [social/individual] media can give them a much better idea of what their lives are actually like. And then they can see more that they are human, just like the rest of us."

（→ 先 Explain 這一段，然後才接 Michael Jackson 的例子。）

- Joe 對 MJ、Diana 例子內容的真實性有些個人保留（accusations、Diana 沒繫安全帶等），但明確說：「The examiner won't think about that anyway, so it doesn't matter.」——**例子的事實準確度不影響評分**，不用為此焦慮。

### Body 2（Princess Diana）— 這篇最強的部分

- 「Good reasons. I like your example as well. Good point, explanation, example. Well done.」
- Point → Explanation → Example 的鏈完整，Joe 直接點名稱讚。
