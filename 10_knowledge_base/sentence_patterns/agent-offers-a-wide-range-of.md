# Sentence Pattern

`[Institution/place] offers a wide range of [plural noun]`

### Function

用「機構／地方當主詞做動作」開句，取代中文式的「在某時間／地方存在著東西」
（There are various X in Y time）。英文偏好讓 agent 當主詞。

### Structure

`[Schools / Universities / The city / The internet] + offer(s) a wide range of [subjects / courses / services / opportunities].`

- 變體：`[Agent] provides / offers students a variety of [X]`
- ⚠️ 修正自 2026-07-13 原稿：`There are various subjects in school time` ❌
  （「在學校時期」直譯；time 不能這樣用，而且句子沒有 agent）

### Usage

Intro 或 Body 開頭的背景句。自我檢查：寫出 `There are... in... time` 這種形狀時，
先問「這個動作是誰做的？」把那個人／機構放回主詞位。

### Example

Schools offer a wide range of subjects, from science and technology to art and music.

### IELTS Topics

- Education · Technology · City life — 任何需要背景句的 Task 2
