## 題目

Some people say that it is better to work for a large company than a small one. Do you agree or disagree?

## 我的草稿

Working in a multinational company seems a dream job to most people. Many graduates tends to find a job from well-known companies, like facebook, Microsoft or other famous cooperations, while private firms are less attractive to them. I partially agree with this opionion for the two main reasons.

First and foremost, large companies have more resources in many aspects, such as funding to research and develop, taking care of employee well-beings and more compliant with operational regulations. As empolyees in these companies are well protected under a organised system. Everything follows proper guidelines and predefined plans. It is unlikely not get paid or work in a unsafety place. In addition, it is likely to offer more opportunities to explore new projects with higher budgets.

In contrast,working in a small firm tends to have more chances to become a piorneer of a startup company. While a company is small, job boundries are vague and allows personal contributions more visible to orgainsation. Once a big success for a project or idea, you can gain more benefits with more flexible way. For example, having shares of stock or become a core member. Working in a small company is like to taking a higher risk but might gain higher rewards. And, that is all depends on your contribution to company's profit.

Despite the potential benefits of working in a small company, it is obvious that being a employee of large companies could gain instant and direct rewarding. In general, work for a well-known companies outweight working in a small company.

## 切題與結構分析

**題型判斷：** 這題是 `Do you agree or disagree?` → Agree/Disagree (Opinion)，你判斷正確。

**問題核心：立場沒有真正執行，讀起來像列優缺點，不是論證。**

你在開頭說「I partially agree」，但整篇的實際走法是：
- Body 1：大公司的優點（資源、保障、機會）
- Body 2：小公司的優點（成為 pioneer、股份、更高風險更高報酬）
- Conclusion：突然一面倒選大公司比較好

這個結構問題出在——依你筆記 `writing_notes.md` 的 Agree/Disagree 標準結構，Body 2 應該是「第二個支持你立場的理由」或「承認對立觀點但反駁它」，而不是單純把小公司的優點也講一遍、完全不反駁。Body 2 讀起來像是在幫小公司辯護，跟你最後選大公司的結論是矛盾的——考官看不出來你到底站哪邊、為什麼。

這正好對照 `06_error_database/error_log.md` 的 Task 2 檢查清單：「是明確選邊 + 給理由，還是只在列優缺點？」→ 這篇目前偏向列優缺點。

**修法：** 如果要維持「partially agree」，Body 2 結尾要有一句真正的 [Result]，說明「即使小公司有這些優點，為什麼整體來說還是不夠強大到贏過大公司」——現在的結尾「that is all depends on your contribution」只是一個模糊的片段，沒有做到這個反駁的工作。

**段落展開鏈（Point → Explain → Support → Result）：**

- **Body 1**：有 Point + Explain，但完全沒有具體例子（沒有情境、沒有假設人物、沒有實際案例），Support 只停在「不太可能領不到薪水/在不安全的地方工作」這種抽象敘述，沒有到 Result 層級。
- **Body 2**：有一個具體例子（「having shares of stock or become a core member」——這個做得對），但段落結尾是一個沒有收好的片段句，沒有連回主論點，違反 `writing_patterns.md` §6 的規則：「Never end a paragraph with an example / 段落不能用沒力的片段結尾」。

## 具體修改建議

1. **先解決立場問題**：這題是單純 Agree/Disagree，不是 Discuss Both Views。如果你要「partially agree」，Body 2 一定要有一句真正反駁/限縮小公司優點的 [Result]，不能只是平行列出小公司的好處就結束。如果嫌 partial 立場難寫，也可以直接選一邊（例如整篇都支持大公司），Body 2 改成「承認小公司優點但說明為什麼還是比不上」——這樣結構更乾淨。
2. **Body 1 加一個具體例子**：現在整段是抽象敘述，缺 Support。可以用一個假設情境（例如：某人在大公司 vs 小公司遇到財務危機時的差別）補上。
3. **Body 2 結尾補一句 [Result]**，把「higher risk higher reward」這個點跟你整體的立場（同不同意大公司比較好）連起來，不要用片段句收尾。
4. **注意冠詞 a/an**：這次同一篇裡出現 3 次，寫完後花 10 秒專門檢查一次「後面接母音開頭字時有沒有用對 a/an」。
