## Expression

damage inflicted on

### Function

Describe serious harm caused to a person, community, system, or environment.

### Usage

Use this formal expression when emphasising the harmful result of an activity or
policy. It is particularly suitable for IELTS Writing conclusions and impact
statements.

`Inflict` normally collocates with negative nouns such as `damage`, `harm`, `pain`,
or `suffering`. Use `on` before the affected person or thing.

### Common Collocations

- damage inflicted on the environment
- harm inflicted on local communities
- suffering inflicted on civilians
- damage inflicted by mass tourism

### Example

The revenue generated by the project cannot justify the damage inflicted on local
ecosystems.
